# Privacy notice

This site (`jameslanzon.com`) is a static portfolio. No backend stores
your data. The only optional analytics is **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**,
loaded **only after** you click **Accept** on the consent prompt.

## What we collect (only with your consent)

- The page path you visited (`/`, `/legal/privacy`, …) and the page
  title, with all query-string parameters stripped except the marketing
  attribution params (`utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`,
  `utm_term`, `utm_content`).
- Your referring URL (HTTP referrer, also redacted to remove query
  strings and fragments).
- Country (derived by Google from your IP — your IP itself is
  **anonymised** before storage; we set `anonymize_ip: true`).
- Device category (mobile / desktop / tablet) and browser family.
- A first-party `_ga` cookie used to attribute return visits.

## What we do NOT collect

- Your precise IP address.
- Advertising identifiers — Google Signals and ad personalization are
  **disabled** at the SDK level (`allow_google_signals: false`,
  `allow_ad_personalization_signals: false`).
- Cross-site identifiers, fingerprints, or third-party tracking.
- Form input, scroll-pixel positions, mouse movements, session
  recordings, or heatmaps.
- Email addresses or any URL query parameters that might contain
  personal data — these are stripped before any analytics call.

## Retention

GA4 retains event-level data for the default **14 months**, after which
it is aggregated. The `_ga` cookie expires after **2 years** unless you
revoke consent first.

## Your controls

- **Cookie preferences** (button at the top of this page) — open the
  prompt at any time to change your decision.
- Revoking consent immediately removes the GA4 script from the page,
  clears the `_ga*` cookies on this domain, and stops all subsequent
  analytics calls.
- We honour the `Sec-GPC` (Global Privacy Control) header and the
  `navigator.doNotTrack === '1'` setting as automatic decline signals.
  When either is on, the consent prompt is hidden by default and the
  GA tag never loads.
- You can also use your browser's content-blocking extensions; we do
  not detect or circumvent them.

## When JavaScript is disabled

The site is fully usable without JavaScript. With JS off, the consent
prompt does not render and no analytics runs.
